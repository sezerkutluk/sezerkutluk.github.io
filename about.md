I am a PhD candidate at Electrical-Electronics Engineering Department, Istanbul University, and a Senior Research Engineer at Huawei Turkey R&D Center. My research topics span probabilistic modeling, Bayesian inference, probabilistic graphical models, deep learning, sparse approximations, and computer vision.

For my PhD thesis, I have been working on probabilistic models for the spectral-spatial classification of hyperspectral images, under the supervision of [Prof. Dr. Aydın Akan](https://scholar.google.com.tr/citations?user=WSG0eK4AAAAJ&hl=en) and [Assoc. Prof. Dr. Koray Kayabol](https://scholar.google.com.tr/citations?user=lIuXJKEAAAAJ&hl=en). In this problem, a segmentation map is obtained by pixel-wise classification. Spectral-spatial classification methods make use of both the spectral signatures and the context. For spectral modeling, I have used mixture models, sparse dictionary learning, and convolutional neural networks, and and for the contextual classification, I have used conditional random field and autologistic regression so far. Moreover, I have used ensemble learning techniques such as bagging and the Dawid-Skene model.

I pursued my MSc degree from Istanbul Technical University in Biomedical Engineering under the supervision of [Prof. Dr. Bilge Günsel](https://scholar.google.com.tr/citations?user=ZxQ_Pm8AAAAJ&hl=en). My thesis was about tissue density classification in mammographic images using local features. During master's studies I was a member of the [Multimedia Signal Processing and Pattern Recognition Lab.](http://www.mspr.itu.edu.tr) of ITU, and I took part in several projects on image/video processing and classification.

I got my bachelor's degree in Electrical-Electronics Engineering with an emphasis on telecommunications from Istanbul University.

At Huawei, I do research and development on machine learning, computer vision, and video processing. Currently I am working on an in-video advertising system and focused on -mostly deep learning based- image/video content understanding and low level image/video processing.

Prior to this, I was a Software Design Engineer at Netaş Telecommunications in Istanbul where I developed embedded signal processing software for satellite navigation.

Apart from all these, I am highly interested in Bayesian deep learning, approximate inference, likelihood-free methods, probabilistic programming, causal inference, signal decomposition, and time-frequency representations.

My publications can be reached through my [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=srwGJWcAAAAJ).
